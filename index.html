On Duty Mandatory tasks!

    Note:

    The Critical Task 1 - Must Do Daily! must be performed first thing upon arriving in the office. The Critical Task 2 - Must Do within the 4-day work week! , review files that monitor tasks with expiration dates and should be raised or actioned accordingly.

    Our objective is to Raise Awareness of items that require attention so that the team can collectively address the problem. As the saying goes "A known problem, is a problem half solved". This can be done by making entries in either the DXB/DWC Station Handover Log, Stores handover log, and/or send email or whatsapp message as necessary.

    "Failure to report, is failing a task!"

Critical Task 1 - Must Do Daily!

  • 1) Review Office Handover Logbook.
  • 2) Review Office Emails & Telex.
  • 3) Check Station Flight Log Display, and ensure all arriving flights are covered.
  • 4) Perform Office Daily Check.
  • 5) Check consumable boxes, and replenish them as necessary.
  • 6) Replenish vehicles with consumable fluids (hydraulic & engine oil).
  • 7) Charge Wireless Headset batteries.
  • 8) Prepare the aircraft check sheets CX and customers.
  • 9) Ensure Ipads(apps updated), laptops, and cellphones are fully charged.
